# Decision Log

## 2026-06-24 — Repository created

ApexCrest repository created as the source of truth for the brand, product, visual, VAT-safe design, commercial, and launch systems.

## 2026-06-24 — ApexCrest visual system locked

ApexCrest is defined as 10 emotional landscapes, not 10 fantasy worlds.

Core production style locked as:

**Premium atmospheric emotional landscapes with restrained book-page echoes.**

Mandatory laws:

- Place Law
- Emotion Law
- Echo Law

## 2026-06-24 — VAT-safe design principle locked

The product must be designed as an illustrated collector book first.

Card placement must be secondary and incidental.

Avoid album, binder, holder, storage, display-accessory, scrapbook, record-book, and stationery framing.

## 2026-06-27 — A5 wallet prototype changed the book structure

The physical A5 prototype showed that the wallet/card occupies more page space than expected once gutter/spine space is accounted for.

Meaningful text cannot sit neatly to the left or right of the card on an A5 display page.

Decision:

- no side text beside the wallet/card
- no writing fields, prompts, lines, or workbook behaviour
- authored world text must live on separate book pages
- each world now uses a 4-page / 2-spread structure
- target first-edition page count moves to 48–56 pages, with 56 preferred
- 10 centred card display pages maximum

Locked 4-page world rhythm:

1. quiet world entry page
2. authored world reading page
3. full world illustration page
4. centred card display page

The full illustration must face the centred card display page to preserve the strongest customer and marketing hero spread.

The opening spread must remain book-first, so the chapter begins with world identity and authored reading matter before the card display appears.

## 2026-06-27 — Visual register rule locked

Each world now has two intensity registers, not two different styles.

Quiet register:

- used for world entry and reading pages
- restrained, readable, premium, and world-specific
- must use the same palette, border logic, texture language, and emotional identity as the hero spread

Hero register:

- used for full illustration and card display spread
- cinematic, atmospheric, and higher intensity
- still part of the same world system

A pattern interrupt is allowed in intensity, not in style.
