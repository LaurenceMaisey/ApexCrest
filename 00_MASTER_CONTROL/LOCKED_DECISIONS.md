# Locked Decisions

This file records decisions treated as current source-of-truth unless deliberately reopened.

## 1. Brand name

**ApexCrest** is the current chosen brand name.

## 2. Core product category

ApexCrest is a **premium illustrated collector book** brand.

## 3. Core phrase

**For the cards that earned their place.**

## 4. Language system

Use:

- Crest
- Crested
- Crest-worthy
- Cresting

Avoid overusing these terms in ways that imply the product is primarily a card-mounting or storage system.

## 5. Visual system

ApexCrest is not 10 fantasy worlds.

ApexCrest is **10 emotional landscapes expressed as premium atmospheric environments**.

## 6. Mandatory visual laws

### Place Law

Every world must be a place someone could imagine entering.

If it is only an effect, reject it.

### Emotion Law

Each world must express one dominant feeling.

If worlds overlap emotionally, redesign one.

### Echo Law

Every right-hand page must carry a subtle visual echo of the left page.

Not a full continuation.
Not generic decoration.
A controlled echo.

## 7. VAT-safe product law

The card placement must remain secondary.

The book must feel complete and valuable before any card is inserted.

## 8. First edition structure direction

Current preferred direction:

- 10 card pages maximum
- additional illustrated/story pages
- approximately 38–44 pages total
- card placement area kept clearly below HMRC risk thresholds

## 9. No character rule

World illustrations must not include franchise characters, franchise lookalikes, mascots, trainers, heroes, villains, or creatures as focal points.

The worlds must remain universal across TCGs and collectible categories.
