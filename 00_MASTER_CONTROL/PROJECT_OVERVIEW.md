# Project Overview

## Project

**ApexCrest**

## Core category

Premium illustrated collector books.

## Core proposition

ApexCrest gives meaningful cards and collectibles a place of recognition inside premium illustrated emotional landscapes.

A binder stores a collection.
ApexCrest honours the cards that earned their place.

## Current first product direction

**The ApexCrest Book**

A hardback illustrated collector book with:

- 10 emotional worlds
- 10 card placement pages maximum for the first edition
- additional illustrated/story pages to strengthen book value and VAT-safe positioning
- restrained right-page world echoes
- premium atmospheric illustration style

## Non-negotiable strategic direction

ApexCrest must remain a book-first product.

It must not drift into being:

- a card album
- a binder
- a storage product
- a display accessory
- a scrapbook
- a record book

## Core phrase

**For the cards that earned their place.**

## Brand language

- Crest
- Crested
- Crest-worthy
- Cresting

Use this language to express recognition and status, but do not let it make the product sound like a card-mounting system.

## Immediate objective

Complete the visual and structural rule system quickly enough to support a four-week illustration production sprint.
