# Book Structure

## Status

Locked current first-edition structure.

## Core structure

**10 Worlds. 10 card slots.**

The first ApexCrest collector book must contain one card slot per world.

This keeps the product:

- physically practical
- visually premium
- emotionally focused
- easier to manufacture
- stronger as a book-first/VAT-safe product
- clearer for customers to understand

## Locked rule

Each world receives:

- one primary world identity
- one emotional theme
- one card slot
- one controlled right-page world echo
- supporting reading matter

## Why 10 card slots

10 card slots is the correct first-edition limit because it avoids the product becoming:

- a binder
- a storage album
- a card-mounting product
- physically bulky when full
- repetitive in page rhythm

The card slots must feel selective.

ApexCrest is for the cards that earned their place.

## Book-first structure

The book should contain additional illustration and story pages beyond the card-slot pages.

These pages exist to make the book feel complete and valuable even before any cards are added.

## Target page count

Current target:

- approximately 38–44 pages total
- 10 card-slot pages maximum
- additional illustrated/story pages used to create rhythm, value and VAT-safe book character

## Card-slot rule

Card slots are special moments, not repeated storage positions.

No additional card slots may be added without reopening the product architecture decision.
