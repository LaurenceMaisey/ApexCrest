# World Theme System

## Status

Locked current production map.

ApexCrest is not 10 fantasy worlds.

ApexCrest is 10 emotional landscapes expressed as premium atmospheric environments.

## 1. Ember Tribute

- Dominant emotion: Passion
- Landscape: Volcanic valley
- Visual language: embers, ash, molten cracks, warm haze
- Echo: drifting embers, ash flecks, molten border detail
- Avoid: dragons, battle scenes, creature silhouettes

## 2. Lagoon Celebration

- Dominant emotion: Joy
- Landscape: Tropical water haven
- Visual language: coral, shells, water lines, sunlight on water
- Echo: shells, coral, ripples, waterline border
- Avoid: pirate clutter, overt adventure props

## 3. Forest Remembrance

- Dominant emotion: Memory
- Landscape: Ancient woodland path
- Visual language: roots, vines, filtered light, old trees
- Echo: vines, roots, leaves, soft woodland texture
- Avoid: fairy woodland, overly cute treatment

## 4. Moonlight Discovery

- Dominant emotion: Mystery
- Landscape: Moonlit ruins and water
- Visual language: moonlit branches, violet mist, reflections, hidden paths
- Echo: moonlit branches, violet mist, silver linework
- Avoid: horror, gothic overload

## 5. Prism Harmony

- Dominant emotion: Beauty
- Landscape: Valley of Light
- Visual language: refraction, convergence, soft rainbow haze, water reflection
- Echo: refracted light, pastel rays, crystal dust
- Avoid: crystal kingdom, gemstone cave, rainbow explosion

## 6. Storm Ascent

- Dominant emotion: Challenge
- Landscape: Storm coast / lightning pass
- Visual language: violent sky, wind, waves, pressure, lightning veins
- Echo: storm clouds, lightning veins, rain texture
- Avoid: snowy mountains

## 7. Frost Resolve

- Dominant emotion: Endurance
- Landscape: Frozen lake / ice valley
- Visual language: stillness, silence, breath, cold, pale light
- Echo: frost crystals, frozen branches, ice texture
- Avoid: heroic mountain climb, epic summit framing

## 8. Celestial Wonder

- Dominant emotion: Awe
- Landscape: Night lake beneath the Milky Way
- Visual language: Orion’s Belt, galaxy dust, star field, vast sky
- Echo: stars, galaxy dust, Milky Way bleed
- Avoid: astrology wheels, observatories, sci-fi structures, giant planets

## 9. Summit Achievement

- Dominant emotion: Triumph
- Landscape: Golden mountain path above clouds
- Visual language: upward path, sunrise, arrival, stonework, alpine plants
- Echo: stonework, alpine plants, golden mist
- Avoid: ice/frost dominance

## 10. Sanctuary Legacy

- Dominant emotion: Belonging
- Landscape: Warm refuge / hidden hall / candlelit garden
- Visual language: ivy, lanterns, warm gold, shelter, memory
- Echo: ivy, lanterns, warm light, soft wood texture
- Avoid: tomb heaviness, temple heaviness, dark shrine energy

## Differentiation rule

If two worlds begin to feel visually or emotionally similar, redesign one immediately.

Storm = movement and danger.
Frost = stillness and endurance.
Summit = arrival and triumph.
Prism = beauty through light, not crystal spectacle.
Celestial = awe through scale, not astronomy clutter.
